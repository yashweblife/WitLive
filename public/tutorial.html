Step 1
Configuring Your Blox
You can skip this step if you dont have the wit-ai blox.
Turn on your blox, and connect your phones wifi to it. The Name you are looking for id Test1, a password will not be required. Once a connection is established, A webpage will promptly open, if it doesnt, type in 192.168.4.1 into your browser and it should pass you to the page. Click Configure Wifi and select your home network, Put your password in. Hit save and you should be good.
Step 2
The Website
This website acts as the eyes and brains for your blox, To use it, Allow permission to use camera.
Go to Trouble shooting for assistance if the permisson block doesnt show up. The video being taken exists only on your browser and dosent record.
Once the camera view loads, it will show you the detection result right below. If you click on the "See What I Can Identify" button, you can see all the labels in the current model. You can choose you labels for your blox by selecting on of the 4 numbers below each label. To get an output on your blox, Enter your auth token into the box and click connect. This will show your corrosponding labels. Enter a label name or directly choose from the "See what I Can Identify" box, Then hit set labels. Now your blox should start working.
Step 3
Make Your Own Labels
The button below will take you to the Teachable Machines website.
TEACHABLE MACHINES
Once you have trained your model, hit exprot, You will see a button saying upload to cloud. Click that and then when the url is available, copy it. On our website, next to the camera button, you will see the settings button, Click that and paste the url into the box. this the button and you should be good to go.
Trouble Shooting
Camera not working
For mobile
If you dont see the allow camera pormpt pop up, go to the browser settings and look for site settings, inside it, click camera and check if this website is present in the allowed section. If it isnt present, clear your browsing history. Reload the page and it should work.
